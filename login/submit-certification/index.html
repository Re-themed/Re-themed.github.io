Submit Your Certification

Learn how to download your certificate for CIW Internet Business Associate and share them via OneDrive.

Step 1:

Create a CIW Login

  1. Navigate to https://i7lp.integral7.com/ciw.
  2. Click on the link labeled "I have a CIW Score Report".
  3. Enter your first and last name as well as your test date.
  4. Enter your Registration Number (ask Coach Lightfoot) and the Site ID: CIW1475 (click to copy), then push "Submit".
Step 2:

Account Setup

  1. Set your username and password to your student username and password (the ones you use to log in to the computers with).
  2. On the left-hand sidebar, under the "Home" header, click "Personal Info".
  3. In the form the appears, enter ONLY your Birth Date.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Submit".
Step 3:

Candidate Agreement

  1. On the left-hand sidebar, under the "Logout" header, click "Logout".
  2. Once you are on the homepage, log in using your student username and password from earlier.
  3. After loggin in, click on "Candidate Agreement" (on the left-hand sidebar, under the "Home" header)
  4. Read and accept both of the presented agreements. Then, on the left-hand sidebar, click "Printable Documents".
Step 4:

Download your Certificate

  1. On the row with "CIW Internet Business Associate eCertificate", click "Print Now". (Note: Make sure that pop-ups are allowed from the site)
  2. Download the PDF that opens in the pop-up window.
  3. After the download is complete, click the upwards-facing arrow and select "Show in Folder".
Step 5:

Uploading Your Certificate

  1. Navigate to https://office.com/leonschools.net.
  2. Login using your student credentials. (Go downwards to learn more)
  3. Click on the OneDrive applet.
  4. Open the File Explorer window from earlier.
  5. Drag the certificate file (it should be highlighted) from the File Explorer window to OneDrive.
Step 5.5:

Loggin in to Office 365

  • When logging in, your username is your student ID (171XXXXXX) followed by "@edu.leonschools.net".
  • Your password is the same one that you use to login to the computers here every day.
  • To bookmark a link for loggin in, drag this red text to your Bookmarks Bar: Office 365.
Step 6:

Sharing Your Certificate

  1. After uploading the ceritification, right click it and select "Share".
  2. Where it asks for and email, enter lightfootj2@leonschools.net (click to copy).
  3. In the mesage spot, enter the subject you got certified in (e.g. "Multimedia", or "Programming").
  4. Click the blue "Send" button.
  5. You're finished!
For Future Reference:

Sharing Files via OneDrive

  • You can use the steps on the previous slide(s) to share almost any other document.
  • Remember to replace Coach Lightfoot's email with the email of your file's intended viewer. To get your teacher's email, go to the Deerlake Teacher Homepage, and navigate to you teacher's website, where you should find their email.
  • Also, you can change the message to whatever you like. Please note that your teacher will receive this message in the form of an email, so go crazy!
  • You can also click "Get Link" instead of "Share" if you just want a link to give out to others. You can also customize how others can view this file when using the link. Also, you can URL shortners like bit.ly can make it a much easier to share that long, complicated share URL.
